Ultimate Guide

Many new players are confused: With so many games in the store, which one should I play?

Mr. Duck was dazzled too. After all, VR is a brand new experience, completely different from PC. With game prices ranging from $9.9 to $50, you definitely want to spend your money on the right titles!

What is Horizon+ ?

First Month Free

New users get their first month free! If you bought the Quest 3 512GB version, Meta also throws in an extra three months of Meta Horizon+ for free!

Monthly Hits

Get two handpicked games every month to redeem for free. As long as you stay subscribed, these games remain in your library for unlimited play.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open Phone App → 2. Enter Menu

"It all starts within the mobile app!"

3

Subscribe and Start Trial

Go to the Meta Horizon+ section, tap "Start Trial," and after linking your card, you'll be set for a free month of play!

Worried about being charged? You can "cancel immediately" after subscribing, and the free trial will still last for the full month!

4

Manually "Redeem" Games

Subscribing doesn't mean games are added automatically. You must visit the Meta Horizon+ section and click "Redeem" for each game to add them to your library!
