SECTOR 7-G
Tactical Map Overview
OP-01

Incoming Storm

Raid on Golis Town Stronghold

MISSION BRIEFING:

We have been monitoring the Golis Town stronghold for a month. The primary objective is to **acquire Human Intelligence (HUMINT)**. Search for hard drives, documents, or USBs in the target building at the end of the street.

Hostiles are local militia, poorly equipped but they use the terrain well. The town recently flooded, causing structural damage. Watch your step. Infil via airdrop at 19:00 under storm cover.

Once complete, signal at the extraction point and prepare for enemy counter-attack during exfil.

Primary Objectives
  • Infiltrate and retrieve the hard drive from the hostile building
  • Reach the designated Extraction Point
Level Tips
Basic Warm-up

Follow the path to the last house, find the hard drive in the 2F room. Once acquired, carefully retreat to near the insertion point.

Bomb Plant

Hidden Forest

Chemical Weapons Facility Sabotage

MISSION BRIEFING:

ISI has located this chemical weapons facility controlled by 'The Jinn'. All internal workers have been replaced by armed personnel.

Mission: Infil via helicopter into the valley, plant C4 explosives at the designated weak points of the facility, and exfil. The chemicals are highly unstable; the explosion will be massive. Get clear once armed.

Hostiles are a mix of mercenaries and militia, highly aggressive. Recommend using high ground for recon and low profile infiltration. No civilians inside; all 'workers' are armed threats. Prepare to engage.

Good luck.

Mission Objectives
  • Locate designated explosive plant sites and destroy the facility
  • Exfil safely upon completion
Tactical Advice

A larger map. Push towards the sheet metal factory after starting. Use the map on your left hand (Map) to confirm the red circle bomb zones.
Note: You only have 1 minute after arming the bomb. Evacuate to a safe distance to avoid KIA. The extraction point (green circle) only appears after the explosion and requires half the squad to enter to complete.

Bomb Defusal

VOID

Bank Gas Bomb Defusal

MISSION BRIEFING:

Supporters of 'The Jinn' have raided a bank and planted chemical weapons. Mission Objective: **Defuse the Chemical Bombs** and clear hostiles.

Power has been cut. Expect 15+ hostiles, fully armed. Recommend disguise and entry via SWAT truck. Civilians have been evacuated.

Infiltration Routes:
1. Rear Employee Entrance / 2. Sewer Manhole / 3. Skylight Rappel / 4. Front/Side Door Assault

Mission Objectives
  • Locate and Defuse any bombs
  • Eliminate hostile threats
  • Example Objectives
  • Proceed to Extraction Point
Tactical Tips

Bomb locations are random (1F/2F/3F/Roof/Basement Vault).
Note: Do not detonate bombs during breaching or firefights. Extraction is usually at the main entrance.

Defusal Protocol

Tutorial Video
  1. Acquire Wire Cutters: From SWAT truck or loadout.
  2. Open Cover: Grip the green bomb cover to reveal wires.
  3. Observe Wires: Wire colors will change (sometimes two at once).
  4. Cut Changing Wire: Cut the wire that changed color last before it changes again. Correct cut produces a high-pitched beep.
  5. Note: Must cut correctly 3 times. Each mistake deducts 2 seconds. Total time 60 seconds.

* Tip: No fall damage in this game. You can break glass on the roof and jump to the basement for a rappel-like experience.

HVT Assassination

SEVER SNAKE

Target Identification & Elimination

ISA INTELLIGENCE BRIEF:

At 0700 hours today, Jovan Adamsovic—advisor to the Hermes Group—will meet with 'The Jinn' members in the abandoned Sudanese town of Bir Qalil. He is being escorted through the town. Expect 30+ hostiles.

Target Profile (PID Required):
Hair: Unknown / Eyes: Brown / Beard: Brown, Full Beard
* Note: Target has two body doubles. Must suppress/kill the masked bald guards first and unmask them to confirm if it is the bearded target.

Mission objective is the precision elimination of Adamsovic. Extraction opens only after positive ID and successful execution. If escalation occurs, massive enemy reinforcements may intervene from outside town.

Environmental Report: Town is in low-lying terrain, open to the east. Weather is hot with ample light. Insertion at 0700, exfil by 1500 latest.

Primary Objectives
  • Locate and Eliminate HVT (PID Required)
  • Eliminate all encountered hostiles
  • Proceed to Extraction Point
Action Mechanism (Important)

Positive ID: Bodies of suspected targets won't disappear. You must manually unmask them. The correct target (bearded) will trigger a success sound and reveal the extraction point. Killing doubles has no effect.

HVT & Data Theft

TRIDENT LIGHTNING

Beach Villa Assassination & Data Theft

ISA INTELLIGENCE BRIEF:

We are deploying your squad to the South China Sea. Target is Brian Gould—philanthropist by day, account manager for Nexa Europa (ISI bomb making facility) by night. Arrest or neutralize immediately.

According to Black Team intel, Gould's mansion houses a computer with extensive transaction data. It requires his **Personal Pass Key** to activate, which he keeps on his person.

Enemy forces consist of 'The Jinn' lower-tier members and elite 'Hermes Group' security. You need to cross the courtyard to the main north building. Target location unknown, assess on site.

Insertion at 1200, complete by 1230. Suit up immediately.

Primary Objectives
  • Locate and Neutralize HVT (Brian Gould)
  • Acquire Pass Key
  • Activate Computer & Upload Data (Defend)
  • Exfil from AO
Tactical Tips

Data Upload Defense: Upload takes 1 minute. Hostiles will counter-attack to destroy the computer. Defend the computer at all costs until upload is complete.
Extraction is at the front garden.

Crash Site & HVT

HAMMER FIRE

Crash Site Demolition & Leader Decapitation

MAYDAY BRIEFING:

Support helo SCEPTRE 2-3 crashed in Hasib Bin-Zara's compound carrying classified data. ISI intel confirms no friendlies survived.

Mission Change: Plant C4 on the wreckage to destroy classified data. Then enter the compound and eliminate Hasib Bin-Zara.

Hostiles are a mix of 'The Jinn' and 'Hermes Group' mercenaries, digging in. The entire town is occupied. Stay alert on approach.

Primary Objectives
  • Destroy Crashed Helo (C4)
  • Eliminate HVT (Hasib Bin-Zara)
  • Exfil from AO
Lethal Mechanism (Important)

Suicide Bomber: Target Bin-Zara is strapped with a bomb. Do NOT shoot the body or it will detonate. After elimination, the bomb initiates a 5-10 second countdown. You must quickly use wire cutters to cut the **RED WIRE** on his chest.

Hostage Rescue

COLLAPSING STAR

Diplomat Rescue Operation

RESCUE OPERATION:

Cartel cell 'Los Cráneos Rojos' is holding diplomat LASCAM hostage at a gas station in Mexico. Rescue authorized.

Intel suggests heavy LCR presence, well-armed and trained. Target is a small gas station, ready for a firefight.

Rules of Engagement (ROE): The only civilian on site is the hostage LASCAM. Zero tolerance for civilian casualties or mission fails. Identify by lack of weapons.

Primary Objectives
  • Infiltrate gas station and neutralize hostiles
  • Retrieve any intel
  • Ensure civilian safety (KIA fails mission)
Tactical Tips

Retrieved documents must be carried to extraction. Success triggers only when the carrier enters the extraction zone.

Intel Retrieval

FLATTENED VEIL

Border Ranche Intel Raid

INTEL RAID:

We are raiding a remote 'Los Cráneos Rojos' ranch in Querétaro. Intel indicates key operational data is hidden there.

The ranch consists of a barn and a two-story main house. Controlled by cartel elites, heavily defended. Caution: **Tripwire Traps** are deployed in the area. Watch your step.

Primary Objectives
  • Infiltrate Ranch Complex
  • Locate and Retrieve Intel (Folders)
  • Exfil
Threat Warning

Enemy Reinforcements: Retrieving the folder triggers immediate heavy reinforcements (15+). Expect a fierce fighting retreat. Also, watch for low tripwires.

Twin Assassination

SHATTERED EAGLE

Twin Sisters Elimination

TARGET PACKAGE:

Targets are the 'VILLA Sisters' (Ona & Salma), alias 'The Warriors'. Financial core of LCR group. Located in a high-density apartment block in Guerrero city.

Targets wear black. No civilians in the area, but the building is packed with hostiles. This is a head-on collision, no way out.

Primary Objectives
  • Locate and Eliminate both 'Villa Sisters'
  • Locate and Retrieve any Intel
Tactical Warning

Swarm: Eliminating the second sister triggers massive enemy reinforcements flooding the building. Plan your cover or escape route in advance.

Dual HVT & Rescue

CLIPPED WINGS

Slum Dual Leader Hunt & Rescue

OPERATION BRIEFING:

Targets locked: Two key LCR figures: former Ranger traitor Jose Palmas and his deputy Pancho Dominguez (El Muerto).

Hiding in the Tabasco slums, tight terrain, heavy enemy presence. Civilians are hiding indoors.

Special Orders: In addition to eliminating both leaders, you must locate and restrain (rescue) 3 civilians. Evacuate only after both conditions are met.

Primary Objectives
  • Eliminate Jose Palmas & Pancho Dominguez
  • Rescue 3 Civilians (Must restrain)
  • Exfil (Return to insertion)
Tactical Warning

Final Wave: Once both leaders are dead and 3 civilians rescued, massive reinforcements will trigger. Extraction is fixed at the **Original Insertion Point**. The return trip will be perilous.
