Customs Guide EZ WAY

Meta official store usually ships via FedEx. You'll need real-name authentication when it arrives in Taiwan.

Prepare Documents

Name, ID Number, Phone, Email, Registered Address.

Upload ID

Take photos of both sides of your ID and upload them in the app.

Confirmation

When the app prompts for authorization, check the tracking number (FedEx number) and click "Matches Declaration".

Proof of Order

If customs inspects, you might need to provide a screenshot of the Meta order as proof of purchase.

FedEx Hotline

For customs or data issues, contact FedEx Taiwan support: 0800-075-075
